Fatherhood & Family Resilience Initiative

Coaching fathers. Strengthening families.

We walk alongside fathers as they reclaim purpose, stabilize their households, and lead with confidence. Our initiative blends coaching, mentorship, discipleship, and job-readiness supports within a trauma-responsive framework.

Who we serve

The initiative centers those often left out of traditional supports—and the partners who walk with them.

First-time & non-custodial fathers

Guidance for navigating early parenthood, reunification, and co-parenting plans.

Men rebuilding

Mentorship and coaching for men emerging from justice involvement or life transitions.

Youth transitioning from care

Support for young adults aging out of foster care as they establish identity and stability.

Church & community partners

Training, playbooks, and alignment tools for faith-based and grassroots organizations.

Program overview

Each cohort includes structured parenting curricula, mentorship circles, vocational readiness, and discipleship pathways delivered by trained facilitators.

  • Parenting cohorts13-week series covering attachment, regulation, and strengths-based discipline.
  • Mentorship & discipleshipPeer networks and small groups for accountability and spiritual growth.
  • Job readiness & stewardshipPractical coaching on employment, financial stewardship, and leadership at home.

Trauma-informed parenting labs

Fathers practice skills through role-play, feedback, and home assignments to reinforce trust-based care.

Youth mentoring (ages 16–21)

Coaching for young men navigating independence, career pathways, and healthy relationships.

Community partnerships

We co-create referral systems, resource hubs, and policy advocacy with local partners.
