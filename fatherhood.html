Program Overview

The initiative includes structured parenting cohorts, mentorship circles, vocational readiness, and youth mentoring. Curriculum is trauma-informed and delivered by trained facilitators.

  • 13
    13-week parenting cohorts
    Structured sessions focused on parenting skills and family dynamics.
  • 🤝
    Mentorship & discipleship circles
    Peer networks for accountability and growth.
  • 💼
    Job readiness & stewardship
    Practical skills to support family stability.

Trauma‑Informed Parenting

Evidence-based strategies for attachment, regulation, and positive discipline.

Youth Mentoring (16–21)

Support building life skills, goal-setting, and pathways to adulthood.
