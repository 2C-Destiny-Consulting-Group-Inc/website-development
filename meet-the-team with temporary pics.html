Malveria Cox Carter

Malveria is an accomplished senior leader with 15+ years of experience in program management, educational administration, and team development. She has a proven track record of overseeing multi-state operations, managing complex budgets, and driving continuous quality improvement initiatives. Malveria is an expert in staff development, contract negotiation, and organizational leadership with a strong foundation in human resources and psychology. She is a founding member of 2C Destiny Consulting Group.

Malveria is a Certified Child Welfare Supervisor.

MaryEtta P. Clarkson

MaryEtta is a visionary leader with more than 30 years of experience spearheading transformative change across child welfare, nonprofit leadership, and community empowerment. As a founding member of 2C Destiny Consulting Group, she brings together compassionate leadership and deep expertise in AI integration, behavioral health, and systems transformation to strengthen support for at-risk families across the nation. Her work is grounded in a lifelong commitment to equity, purpose-driven innovation, and creating lasting, measurable impact in the communities she serves.

Robert P. Carter

Robert is a previous owner of a licensed emergency shelter and foster group home and an accomplished, dedicated professional with over 25 years of experience in facility management, healthcare administration, and customer service. He has a proven track record of improving operational efficiency, leading teams to achieve organizational goals, and enhancing customer satisfaction. Robert is a founding member of 2C Destiny Consulting Group.

Sonia Lynch-Dillard

Sonia is an expert at turning research into practice. She designs scenario-based curricula and facilitates train-the-trainer cohorts so skills stick under real-world pressure. Her adult-learning approach draws on brain science, coaching, and reflective supervision—giving practitioners tools to lead with empathy and clarity.

Doris “DeeDee” Banks

Doris (known as DeeDee) is a leader who advances process integrity—aligning policies, documentation, and day-to-day practice so families receive consistent, high-quality services. She leads fidelity checks, case reviews, and staff coaching that improve timeliness, accuracy, and follow-through. With a keen eye for detail and a collaborative style, Doris helps teams simplify workflows and adopt practical routines that reduce errors and raise confidence across the line.
