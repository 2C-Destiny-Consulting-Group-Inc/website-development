Malveria Cox Carter

As CEO, Malveria blends lived experience, frontline practice, and executive leadership to advance safe, equitable outcomes across the child- and family-serving ecosystem. She has led multi-site initiatives improving permanency, well-being, and timely access to services by aligning strategy, culture, and supervision. Her practice is anchored in trauma-responsive, culturally grounded approaches and collaboration with families and community partners. Trained in models such as TBRI®, Nurturing Parenting, and the Duluth Model, she champions strengths-based engagement and practical tools teams can use day one.

Strategy & Culture Cross-Agency Partnerships Trauma-Responsive Practice

MaryEtta P. Clarkson

MaryEtta translates research into reliable operations. With a background spanning program evaluation and continuous quality improvement, she helps agencies build data systems that inform decisions, strengthen practice, and demonstrate impact. Her teams deploy practical dashboards, fidelity reviews, and rapid-cycle learning to close equity gaps and sustain results. She specializes in implementation science, evidence synthesis, and staff coaching that makes “evidence-based” real in daily work.

CQI & Evaluation Implementation Science Data-Informed Decisions

Robert P. Carter

Robert builds trust at the neighborhood level—coordinating coalitions, fatherhood engagement, and peer-led supports that move the needle on access and retention. He is known for practical outreach strategies, partnership brokering, and community events that elevate family voice. Robert trains mentors and paraprofessionals to deliver consistent, welcoming experiences from intake through transition, and he collaborates closely with providers to align services with local needs.

Community Partnerships Fatherhood Initiatives Peer Support Networks

Doris “DeeDee” Banks

DeeDee advances process integrity—aligning policies, documentation, and day-to-day practice so families receive consistent, high-quality services. She leads fidelity checks, case reviews, and staff coaching that improve timeliness, accuracy, and follow-through. With a keen eye for detail and a collaborative style, DeeDee helps teams simplify workflows and adopt practical routines that reduce errors and raise confidence across the line.

Quality & Compliance Process Improvement Case Review & Fidelity

Sonia Lynch-Dillard

Sonia turns research into practice. She designs scenario-based curricula and facilitates train-the-trainer cohorts so skills stick under real-world pressure. Her adult-learning approach draws on brain science, coaching, and reflective supervision—giving practitioners tools to lead with empathy and clarity. She collaborates with content experts and community advisors to ensure materials are practical, accessible, and culturally grounded.

Instructional Design Scenario-Based Training Coaching & Facilitation
